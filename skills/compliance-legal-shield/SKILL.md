## Finance & Lending — Required Disclosures

### Must-Have on Every Loan Landing Page
```
Representative APR Example:
"Loan amounts range from $1,000 to $50,000. APR ranges from 5.99% to 35.99%.
Loan terms range from 12 to 84 months. Example: A $10,000 loan at 15% APR
for 36 months has a monthly payment of $346.65 and total cost of $12,479.40."

Not-a-Lender Statement:
"[SiteName] is not a lender and does not make credit decisions. We connect
borrowers with a network of lenders who may offer loans based on the
information you submit."

No Guarantee Statement:
"Submission of a loan request does not guarantee you will be connected
with a lender or approved for a loan."
```

### Footer Disclosure Template (Finance)
```html
<div class="legal-disclosure">

  loan broker. We are an advertising referral service to qualified participating

  all states. Residents of some states may not be eligible for loans.
  Credit checks may be performed by lenders.</p>

  <p>APR Disclosure: The Annual Percentage Rate (APR) is the cost of credit
  as a yearly rate and ranges from [X%] to [X%], which may include an
```

## Nutra/Health — FDA & FTC Requirements

### Required Disclaimer
```
"These statements have not been evaluated by the Food and Drug Administration.
This product is not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any disease."
```

### FTC Rules for Health Claims
- ❌ Never: "Clinically proven to cure..." → ✅ "Formulated to support..."
- ❌ Never: "Doctors recommend..." without documented evidence
- ❌ Never: Before/after images without clear disclaimer
- ❌ Never: "Results typical" — must say "Results not typical. Individual results may vary."
- ✅ Always: Disclose paid testimonials ("Paid endorsement")
- ✅ Always: Disclose affiliate relationships ("We may earn a commission")

### Meta Ads — Nutra Restricted
```
Banned in ads:
- Before/after weight loss images
- "Lose X pounds in Y days"
- Claims of disease treatment/cure
- Exaggerated body transformation language

Allowed:
- "Feel your best" / "Support your wellness journey"
- Lifestyle imagery (active, healthy people)
- Product shots with benefit language ("formulated with...")
```

## Email Compliance (CAN-SPAM + GDPR)

### CAN-SPAM Checklist (US — every email)
- [ ] Accurate From name and email address
- [ ] Honest subject line (no deceptive teasers)
- [ ] Physical mailing address in footer (real address required)
- [ ] Clear unsubscribe mechanism
- [ ] Honor unsubscribe within 10 business days
- [ ] No sending to purchased lists without opt-in proof

### Footer Template (Email — CAN-SPAM compliant)
```
You're receiving this because you requested information about [topic].

[Company Name] | [Physical Address] | [City, State ZIP]

[Unsubscribe] | [Privacy Policy] | [Update Preferences]
```

### TCPA (Text/Phone — critical for lead-gen)
```
Required consent language on forms:
"By clicking Submit, you consent to be contacted by [Company] and its
lending partners at the phone number provided, including by autodialer
or prerecorded calls/texts, even if on a Do Not Call registry.
Consent is not required to receive services. Message and data rates may apply."
```

## Privacy Policy — Key Sections Required

For any site collecting user data:

1. **What data you collect** — name, email, phone, IP, cookies
2. **How you use it** — matching with lenders/offers, marketing
3. **Who you share it with** — lender network, advertising partners
4. **User rights** — access, deletion, opt-out (CCPA: California users)
5. **Cookie policy** — analytics, tracking, remarketing pixels
6. **Contact information** — how users can reach you

### CCPA Notice (California — required if you have CA traffic)
```
"California residents have the right to know what personal information
is collected, request deletion of their data, and opt-out of the sale
of their personal information. To exercise these rights, contact:
[email/link]"
```

## Google Ads Compliance (Finance)

### Certification Requirements
- Personal loans require Google Financial Products certification
- Must display representative APR in ads
- Must have compliant landing page before approval
- Payday loan ads heavily restricted — verify by country

### Ad Copy Rules
```
✅ Allowed:
"Personal Loans from 5.99% APR | Apply in 2 Minutes"
"Check Your Rate — No Credit Impact"

❌ Not allowed:
"Guaranteed Approval" (can't guarantee)
"Instant Cash" (misleading urgency)
"0% Interest Loans" (unless literally true)
```
